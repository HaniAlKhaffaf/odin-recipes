Pizza Recipe


pizza, dish of Italian origin consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with some delicious ingredients

One of the simplest and most traditional pizzas is the Margherita, which is topped with tomatoes or tomato sauce, mozzarella, and basil. Popular legend relates that it was named for Queen Margherita, wife of Umberto I, who was said to have liked its mild fresh flavour and to have also noted that its topping colours—green, white, and red—were those of the Italian flag.

Ingredients
  • Flour
  • Dry Yeast
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil Leaf

Cooking Steps
  1. make the dough by adding yeast to the flour
  2. add salt and olive oil to yeast and leave it for 30 minutes
  3. after the dough is ready put some tomatoe paste on top
  4. put mozzarella on top, better to shred it and spread it evenly
  5. add basil leaf on top
  6. put in oven for 30 minutes under medium heat
  7. take it out and let cool down a bit and it's all ready to serve
