Lasagne Recipe


Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta but with different style and taste.

Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Lasagne Ingredients
  • White Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Beef Mince
  • Red Wine
  • Lasagne Sheets

Cooking Steps
  1. Cook minced meat with the red wine in a fryingi pan
  2. bring a nice pot for cooking and fill the base with lasagne sheets
  3. bring the permesan cheese and fill the layer above the minced meat layer
  4. Now we bring more lasagne sheets and cover the top later again and start the same process
  5. when you finished stacking the lasagne layers. Finish your lasagne by adding the white sauce in the end and make sure it fills the entire pot
